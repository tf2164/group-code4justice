It's okay to need help and reach out for it. Never feel like you're alone in what you are going though. There are always people you can reach out to.

Crisis Text Line by texting TALK to 741741

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline 800-273-8255

Single Stop

------------------------

Single Stop can help you overcome financial or other barriers by connecting you to affordable technology, housing resources, legal assistance, food referrals, healthcare resources, free tax preparation, and more. Single Stop also offers several emergency grants to help you pay for your basic expenses.

Learn more about what single stop can do for you, and click here

Avance Psychiatry

------------------------

About 1 in 4 adults and almost as many adolescents suffer from psychiatric illness at some point in their lives. Unfortunately, signs of psychiatric disorders often go unnoticed, and those who would most benefit from treatment do not receive it.
